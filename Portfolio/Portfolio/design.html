¿How its the Creative process?

Desing something is not easy, the desing process can be kind of frustating the creative block happens but a creative mind can always get out , you just need time and music , like everything theres a path to follow , usually i follow the same pattern when its time to desing to make it more fluent










¿Why its important to follow a path?

Following a path or a map can help us to make us a idea of how the project could be.
Follow a step by step make it so much easier when its time to collaborate with other people , can imporove the efficiency and the comunication in case that you were working with a team. Or just in fact that you can see how its going stage by stage in case that you can get back to change somethig.

❀ Brainstorming

The basic part of the creative process, where you think about the idea and the concept of the project , in this phase i list all the ideas in a paper to concrete and make a roadmap to follow.

×

❀ Sketching

Usually i skecth to make diferents wireframes to see how its going and make my creativity flow to see the diferents posible ways to make the project.

×

❀ Design

In this phase we can finally start desing , but we dont desing in code , first we need to desing in a editor program ,my favorite is figma and penpot.

×

❀ Get a feedback

Once ready the Design in our editor program we can ask some people what they think about it ,so we can recopilate the feedback and make the necesary changes trying to make the best posible design.

×

❀ Correct and design

The last phase, we can finally starting to code following all the corrections made previously
